Haiga Rice

Get ready to enjoy a real treat

A beautiful and nutritious dish that will fill you up and delight the senses!

This dish is easy to make. Follow these steps to find out how.

Here is what you need:

  • Water
  • Haiga rice

How to make it:

  1. Get out the rice
  2. Wash it a few times under water
  3. Put it in the rice cooker
